ABOUT

I’m a multidisciplinary designer specializing in packaging, product, and visual design. My work blends sculptural aesthetics with precise functionality — where every curve, texture, and reflection serves a purpose.

With over a decade of experience, I focus on crafting original concepts from the ground up — modeling, rendering, and composing visuals that balance elegance, clarity, and innovation.

My background combines graphic design, 3D modeling, and product visualization. Over the years, I’ve worked across packaging, showroom, and industrial design — developing projects from initial concept through to presentation and rendering.
